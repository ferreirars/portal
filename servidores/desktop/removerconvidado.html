Remover Convidado

Edite o arquivo: sudo gedit /etc/lightdm/lightdm.conf

original:

[SeatDefaults]
greeter-session=unity-greeter
user-session=ubuntu


Modifique para:

[SeatDefaults]
greeter-session=unity-greeter
user-session=ubuntu
allow-guest=false

Reinicie ou execute: sudo restart lightdm

<a href="http://ftbeowulf.wordpress.com/2011/10/19/how-to-disable-the-ubuntu-11-10-guest-session/">http://ftbeowulf.wordpress.com/2011/10/19/how-to-disable-the-ubuntu-11-10-guest-session/</a>
